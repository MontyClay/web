About

Clayaveras is the First Handcrafted Clay Skeletons NFT Collection on Cardano, with a unique art never seen before, Main focus on art, but the team is constantly adding utility to it. At Clayaveras we want to create a club of friends who share the same vibe and the same interests. Through the art of Clay, we want to offer our community a CNFT space where they feel part of a project with great potential, which is constantly growing, participating in different international events and conferences and always trying to give greater benefits to the holders. We are very grateful for the great support of our community and this is why we are always working to expand our Clayaveras brand and take it to the top. We will be constantly updating our progress. You can take a look at our Claymap to know about our future plans. Join us to write the story of Clayaveras together!
